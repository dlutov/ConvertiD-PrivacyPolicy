# 1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information, primarily to ensure the proper functioning of our App and to enable you to utilize its core features, particularly in relation to social media marketing APIs. The types of information we collect include:


## 1.1. Information You Provide to Us


- **Social Media Platform Credentials**: When you connect your social media advertising accounts to our App, we access necessary credentials, such as API keys, access tokens, or account IDs. These are strictly required by the respective social media platforms' marketing APIs to enable you to publish, manipulate, and manage campaigns, ad sets, and other advertising elements through our App. We collect these credentials solely for the purpose of interacting with these platforms on your behalf, as authorized by you.

- **First-Party Contact Data for Audience Creation**: You may choose to upload or provide first-party contact data (e.g., email addresses, phone numbers) directly to our App for the purpose of creating custom audiences or similar audience segments on social media platforms. This data is processed and transmitted to the respective social media platforms solely for audience targeting as explicitly directed by you, and in compliance with their terms and policies. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

- **Contact Information**: If you contact us for support, inquiries, or feedback, you may provide us with your name, email address, or other contact details. This information is used solely to respond to your requests and provide assistance.

## 1.2. Information Collected Automatically (Analytics Data)

We collect limited data through analytics for the App's proper functioning, continuous improvement, and performance monitoring. This data is processed in an aggregated and anonymized manner, specifically designed to prevent individual user tracking or profiling. The types of automatically collected data include:

- **Usage Information**: Details about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use, the duration of your sessions, clicks, internal links accessed, pages visited, scrolling behavior, searches performed, and timestamps of your activities.

- **Device Information**: Information about the device and browser you use to access the App, including browser type and version, network connection details, device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
Server Information: This includes your Internet Protocol (IP) address.
