# 4. How We Share Your Information

We are transparent about how we share your information with third parties.

## 4.1. Sharing with social media platforms

To enable the core functionality of our App, we share user credentials and campaign-related data with Meta Platforms, Inc. (e.g., Facebook, Instagram) and X platforms to facilitate interaction with their Marketing API. This allows you to publish and manipulate advertising campaigns on their respective platforms. Any data shared with Meta (e.g., campaign configurations, ad set details) is subsequently handled by Meta in accordance with its own privacy policies and terms of service. We encourage you to review Meta's relevant policies:


- [Meta's Data Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/explanation)
- [Meta's Platform Terms](https://developers.facebook.com/terms/)
- [X Privacy Policy](https://x.com/en/privacy)



## 4.2. Other Third Parties 

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for their independent marketing, advertising, or other purposes, unless explicitly consented to by you or required by law.
